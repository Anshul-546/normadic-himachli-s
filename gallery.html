Baba Balaknath Temple, Hamirpur
  •  400 reviews

It is situated 45 KMs. away from “Hamirpur” on the border of Hamirpur and Bilaspur districts of Himachal Pradesh, India. The Baba Balak Nath Temple is situated at the village of “Chakmoh” in the district Hamirpur on hilltop, in a natural cave. An Idol of Baba is placed in the cave.

Norbulingka Institute, founded in 1995 by Kelsang and Kim Yeshi at Sidhpur, near Dharamshala, India, is dedicated to the preservation of the Tibetan culture in its literary and artistic forms.

Bijli Mahadev Kullu
  •  400 reviews

Bijali (thunder Mahadeva temple) Mahadeva Mandir is a located in Kashawri village, Kullu Valley in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. It is located at an altitude of about 2,460m in the Kullu Valley. Bijli Mahadev is one of the ancient temples in India and dedicated to Lord Shiva (Mahadev).

  •  400 reviews

Baba Baroh Temple is a popular tourist destination located approximately 52 km away from Dharamshala. Devoted to the Hindu God Krishna and his spouse Radha, this shrine is visited by many pilgrims everyday.

Shrikhand Mahadev
  •  400 reviews

Shrikhand Mahadev Kailash, also called Shikhar Kailash,[1] is a Hindu pilgrimage site in Nirmand sub-division of Kullu, Himachal Pradesh, India, considered to be an abode of Lord Shiva and his wife Goddess Parvati. It is considered to be one of the toughest treks in India.
